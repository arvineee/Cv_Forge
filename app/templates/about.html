Our Mission

CVForge AI was built with a single conviction: African professionals are world-class, and their CVs should reflect that. Too many talented people lose opportunities not because of their skills, but because their CV fails the first filter — an automated ATS scanner that never sees the human behind the document.

We built CVForge to close that gap. Using the same AI technology that powers Fortune 500 hiring tools, we give every job seeker — from a fresh graduate in Nairobi to a senior engineer in Lagos — the ability to build a CV that gets through the door.

No design skills needed. No expensive career coaches. Just powerful AI, professional templates, and M-Pesa payments that work for everyone.
